Ganpat University

Bringing Premier Education to Your Doorstep

Ganpat University and the township of Ganpat Vidyanagar, a high-tech education campus, is a joint initiative — purely philanthropic in nature — by a large number of industrialists and technocrats, noble farmers and affluent businessmen for the mission of "Social Upliftment through Education." The University was established by the State Government by enactment of Act No. 19/2005 on 12th April 2005, and is approved under Section 2(f) by the University Grants Commission (UGC).

In consideration of its contribution to education in a short span of time, the University has been given Permanent Membership in renowned academic and industry associations, such as the Association of Indian Universities (AIU) and the Association of Commonwealth Universities (ACU).

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Ganpat University Online UGC approved? +×
Yes, Ganpat University Online is a UGC-approved online university authorised to offer online degree programs in India. All degrees awarded are fully recognised nationally and internationally.
What are the BBA fees and eligibility? +×
The Online BBA is a 3-year program with an annual fee of ₹30,500 (total ₹90,500). Eligibility: 10+2 / HSC from any recognised board. Pay full fee upfront and save 20% — pay just ₹77,300.
What are the MBA fees and eligibility? +×
The Online MBA is a 2-year program with an annual fee of ₹49,000 (total ₹98,000 for 2 years). Eligibility: Bachelor's degree from any recognised university with at least 50% aggregate marks (45% for reserved category).
What are the MCA fees and eligibility? +×
The Online MCA is a 2-year program with an annual fee of ₹49,000. Eligibility: Bachelor's degree in Computer Applications, Computer Science, or Mathematics with at least 50% marks.
How are online classes conducted? +×
Classes are delivered through an interactive Learning Management System (LMS) with recorded lectures, live sessions, e-content, practice tests, and virtual academic support available 24/7.
Is the online degree valid for jobs and higher studies? +×
Yes. Degrees awarded by Ganpat University Online are valid for employment and higher education in India and abroad. The university is UGC-entitled and NAAC 'A' accredited.
Are EMI or loan options available? +×
Yes! You can choose from loan assistance, easy EMI plans, and several flexible payment options. Contact our counsellors at admission@guniol.com or +91 9240271367 for personalised support.
